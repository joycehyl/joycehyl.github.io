We are a private organization that aims to preserve specimens of soon extinct rare species. Using this website as a documentation archive, we hope for everyone to witness our nature's beauty. Our team consists of scientists and computer technicians that work correspondingly to perfect our entire specimen archive, feel free to visit our physical lab to see a full gallery view on more than 5000 soon-extinct specimens.
