All creatures are generated by ArtBreeder with specimen bios referenced to Wikipedia——none of the living things on this site is real. This is an art project done by Joyce Ho in 2021, aimed to satirize the efforts done to protect what earth still has left to offer. Instead of preserving the extinction of actual living species, the website aims to capitalize off of extinct species without any attempt to save them. From the beginning of our search for the final frontier in cosmos, people seem to have planted the concept of earth 2.0. Exploiting, invading, and eventually evading from the problems still left on earth 1.0. What has been done to save the earth must be acknowledged, but it may be never be enough…Our time has so much more to compensate.
